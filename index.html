My Poems

Title: The Path To Myselfe

Walking down the lane of time,

Confused—what to be or what not to be?

Mom wishes, Dad wishes,

Siblings' desires—whose dreams to fulfill first?

I want to be me,

But still haven’t found my name yet.

Who am I? What am I?

What are my goals? What dreams are mine?

Are they foolish questions?

Or do I truly need to think of them?

At times, it feels like the answers drift away,

Like leaves in the autumn wind.

Yet, deep within, a quiet voice stirs:

"Take your time, for the path unfolds.

Let your heart guide you,

And you’ll find who you’re meant to be."

Title: princess Treassure

Oh priceless treasure that I’ve found,

Let me be the shoulder for your burdens to lay on.

Let me be your moon,

Shining my light in your universe.

Let me be the one you can seek support from—

The hand to hold when you feel lost.

Through the storms, I’ll be your calm.

Through the darkness, I’ll be your guide.

For you, I’ll be steady,

A pillar when you need strength.

Oh priceless treasure, let me be

All you need, as you are to me.

Title: A Broken Heart Solitude

I have let go,

Even though I was once hurt—

It’s okay now.

I have moved on,

No longer holding on to

Complicated feelings.

I’ve found solitude

In being only me now.

For I no longer feel anything

After looking back at what once was.

The weight has lifted,

The pain has dulled,

And now, all that remains is peace.

Title: Guided By Your Light

I fall over and over again,

Despite trying to guard my heart.

But the more I fall,

The more it leads me towards you.

What magic lies in those eyes of yours?

I see my entire world within them.

I long to hold you closer,

And keep you safely in my arms,

Forever.

Title: The Walls She Built

She’s lost,

In a world of her own,

Slowly and deeply falling

In love with darkness and loneliness.

She’s lost,

Complicated thoughts and fading ideas,

Living in a completely imaginary world.

She’s lost,

With a heart full of complications,

Or maybe stranded emotions.

She’s lost,

In a faraway world

Where no one can reach her,

With no sense of security.

She stands guarded,

Surrounded by walls

She’s built to keep the world away.

Title: The Weight Of A Smile

Smiling, even though it bleeds,

With a pain gripping tight, almost suffocating.

I’m broken, yes, I am,

But I can’t show it—

I have to keep smiling.

Even with tears in my eyes,

My lips will keep smiling continuously.

A mind full of painful pasts,

A life full of loneliness,

A mask of fake happiness.

Tired of begging for love, care, attention.

Tired of being hurt

Over and over again.

Is there no medicine

For a broken heart?

Title: Where Your Shadow Went

Claimed to love me,

But didn’t see your shadow

When I longed for your touch, or maybe just a slight care.

Claimed to always be my shadow,

When I was vulnerable and needed someone to lean on,

Or maybe when I cried in a room as dark as coal,

Wetting my pillow as though I were raining on it.

Claimed to always understand me,

But didn’t see your shadow

When I was yearning for you

On endless, sleepless nights.

Claimed you understood,

But I was left in the silence,

Wondering where your shadow went.
